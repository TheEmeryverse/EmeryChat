[S14] New research finds $15 minimum wages raise pay and increase employment for low-wage workers - Equitable Growth
https://equitablegrowth.org/new-research-finds-15-minimum-wages-raise-pay-and-increase-employment-for-low-wage-workers/
Equitable Growth working paper (Reich, Wiltshire, McPherson et al., Oct 16, 2023) analyzes the impact of $15 minimum wages in California and New York (2013‑2022) using a synthetic‑control method across 47 counties. It finds that the wage hikes raise earnings for low‑wage workers (especially teen and fast‑food employees) and increase employment, with effects concentrated near the new wage. The authors argue that monopsony power depresses wages, and higher minimum wages reduce turnover and profit erosion, yielding positive labor‑market outcomes and supporting government‑mandated wage floors over free‑market determination.
