## Core Findings
1.  **Tactical Escalation Over Infrastructure:** No physical personnel increases or facility expansions have been verified beyond field office activity. The "buildup" consists of heightened enforcement tactics, including unmasked agents and vehicle break-ins at private residences [S1][P5].
2.  **Targeted Residential Detentions:** ICE is conducting active residential arrests in established Milwaukee neighborhoods and Waukesha streets, bypassing traditional processing centers for immediate removal [S4].
3.  **Municipal Pushback:** Both city and county governments are actively resisting enforcement via legislative measures—ordinances requiring agent visibility and resolutions opposing courthouse activity—to protect community trust [S5][S13].

## Competing Interpretations
*   **ICE's Stance (Source S7):** Emphasizes targeting of criminal aliens and violent offenders to prioritize public safety, supported by national initiatives like the VOICE Office and 287(g) partnerships. *(Note: Official source; inherently promotional.)*
